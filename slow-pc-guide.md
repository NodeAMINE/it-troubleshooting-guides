# Guide: How to fix a slow computer

## Symptoms
- Computer takes more than 2 minutes to start
- Programs open very slowly
- Computer freezes often

## Step-by-step troubleshooting

### 1. Check startup programs
1. Press `Ctrl + Shift + Esc` to open Task Manager
2. Click on the "Startup" tab
3. Look for programs with "High" or "Medium" startup impact
4. Right-click on unnecessary programs and select "Disable"

### 2. Clean temporary files
1. Press `Windows + R`
2. Type `%temp%` and press Enter
3. Select all files (`Ctrl + A`)
4. Delete them (skip files that are in use)

### 3. Check disk space
1. Open File Explorer
2. Click on "This PC"
3. Look at the space left on the C: drive
4. If less than 10 GB is free, recommend cleaning or upgrading

## When to escalate to level 2
- Problem persists after following all steps
- Hardware failure suspected (strange noises, overheating)
- Deep virus infection suspected
